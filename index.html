STEMHacks

Programmers today, leaders tomorrow.

event May 6-7 place Downingtown STEM

STEMHacks is one of the very first high-school hackathons in Pennsylvania who’s mission it is to inspire the youth to program. In 24 hours 100+ students from the tri-state area will create a project from scratch that will be judged from some of the leaders in the tech industry for a chance to win amazing prizes. With unlimited food, drink, interesting demos and workshops, and of absolutely no cost, we can’t wait to see you there!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon? arrow_forward
A hackathon is a timed event during which students who share an enthusiasm for technology build cool things, called software or hardware "hacks". Although hackathons are competitions, they're fantastic learning experiences. Click here for more info.
I'm a beginner! arrow_forward
Perfect! STEMHacks is the hackathon for learning how to code, so even if you've never programmed before, don't worry! We'll have plenty of workshops to get you started and mentors to help you out
What should I build? arrow_forward
Many people build games, apps, or websites. However, if you have an idea for something else, you can certainly try making it. If you don't have an idea, that's totally fine too! Either way, our mentors at the workshops can help you with your hack.

Contact us at contact@stemhacks.com for any other questions!!
